About Me

I am a 20 year old guy from Germany and I've been interested in music as a tool for expression since a while, but only in late 2021 I decided to enter the space as a producer myself.

I started it all with a mixture of electronic orchestral music, Lo-Fi and a bit of Chiptune and I am still occasionally experimenting with these genres to this day. 

After a long surge of Retrowave and Synthwave I recently decided to try myself in more than one genre, nowadays I'm primarily making music that combines multiple genres. Unfortunately this often leads to some of my songs being more experimental than presentable.
