About me

Aliases: Mibris and FLΛSHDRV

I am a 21 year old guy from Germany and I've been interested in music as a way of expression since a while, but only in late 2021 I decided to enter the space as a producer myself.

I started it all with a mixture of electronic orchestral music, Lo-Fi and a bit of Chiptune and I am still occasionally experimenting with these genres to this day. 

After a long surge of Retrowave and Synthwave, I decided to explore a bit. Since then I've been working with various genres, but mostly end up with melodic house or melodic dubstep.
